Welcome To Riget Zoo Adventures!

Yorkshire's Best Zoo

Step into the wild world of Riget Zoo Adventures, where nature comes alive! Designed especially for young explorers, our guided tours and interactive experiences bring kids up close with fascinating animals in a fun and educational way.

Explore exotic habitats, meet amazing creatures, and embark on unforgettable journeys through the heart of the animal kingdom. Whether your child is an aspiring zookeeper or just loves wildlife, there's always something new to discover. Start your adventure today!

Plan Your Visit

Open daily from 9 AM to 6 PM, we offer interactive exhibits, guided tours, and feeding sessions that bring you closer to the animal kingdom. Don’t miss our special animal encounters, where you can get up close with incredible species like giraffes, penguins, or sloths. Enjoy a meal at one of our on-site cafes, featuring kid-friendly and sustainable dining options. Visit our gift shop for unique souvenirs that support wildlife conservation. Whether it’s your first visit or your tenth, there’s always something new to experience.

Conservation & Education

We’re dedicated to wildlife conservation and education, partnering with global initiatives to protect endangered species and their habitats. Through our breeding programs and research efforts, we help ensure a future for vulnerable animals. Our educational programs, including school visits, summer camps, and hands-on workshops, inspire the next generation of conservationists. Learn how everyday choices, from reducing plastic waste to supporting ethical tourism, can have a lasting impact on the planet. Together, we can make a difference for wildlife and future generations.

Tickets & Membership

Get your tickets online or at the gate, with flexible pricing options for individuals, families, and groups. Become a member for unlimited visits year-round, exclusive events, and behind-the-scenes access to our animals and their keepers. Membership also includes discounts on food, merchandise, and special experiences like zookeeper chats and VIP tours. Planning a special occasion? We offer birthday party packages and private event spaces to make any celebration truly wild. Supporting the zoo through membership helps fund our conservation efforts and animal care programs.

You aren't Convinced yet?

We get it—you’re skeptical. Maybe you’ve been to zoos that felt more like prisons than sanctuaries. Maybe you think animals belong in the wild, not behind glass. Or maybe you just don’t see why a trip to the zoo should be on your list. But here’s the thing: our zoo isn’t just about putting animals on display. It’s about conservation, education, and creating real connections between people and the natural world. When you walk through our gates, you’re stepping into something much bigger than just a day out.

Our exhibits are designed with the animals’ well-being as the top priority, giving them the space, enrichment, and care they need to thrive. Every ticket you buy helps fund conservation programs that protect wildlife both here and in their natural habitats. Whether you’re here to see majestic predators up close, watch playful primates swing through the trees, or experience hands-on encounters with some of the world’s most fascinating species, there’s something here that will surprise you. More than that, you’ll leave with a deeper appreciation for the creatures we share this planet with.
